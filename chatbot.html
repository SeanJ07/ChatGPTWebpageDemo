Ask me about Medicare
We created a demo GPT builder and copied it to a website that provides free usage. Using GPT, we coded with HTML and learned how to implement it, making the project accessible through GitHub.

This software is an AI chatbot designed for seniors who have questions and concerns regarding their medical or healthcare insurance. It offers a quick and easy alternative to making calls or searching the web for specific answers. The AI's responses are generated based on previously collected and stored data, tailored to meet the specific requests and questions asked by the company's customers. The chatbot can be accessed through a link or QR code.
